Burndown

Purpose

View the progress of individual milestones.

Understanding the Burndown

The portlet displays the progress of the individual milestones. It will tell you, how much work is/was left to do for a given date. The x-axis is a time span, the y-axis is time of work (hours). When choosing a release in the Dependencies, this portlet will show all milestones from the selected release.
